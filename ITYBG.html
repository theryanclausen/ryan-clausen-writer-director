I Thought You'd be Gone

After slipping into a depression following the end of her marriage, Maddie has decided she is ready to move on. Unfortunately, her emotionally abusive ex-husband is not.

This film was shot in Ryan's house in Tucson, Arizona for a budget of $800. I Thought You'd be Gone Premiered at the Seattle Transmedia and Independent Film Festival.
