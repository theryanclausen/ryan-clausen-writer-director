Guilty Shoes

After witnessing a bully take things to far, a high school student takes it upon himself to murder the bully.

This film was shot at Tahoma High School in Maple Valley, Washington. Guilty Shoes was featured at film festivals across the country and won an Audience choice award and the National Film Festival for Talented Youth.
