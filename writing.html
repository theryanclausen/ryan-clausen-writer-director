In addition to writing all of the projects he has directed, Ryan has also written on a few other projects.

Ryan briefly wrote headlines for Tucson's local access "Five on 20 News" program. Some highlights of that endeavor can be seen in the window to the left.

Ryan's unproduced screenplay, Homecoming, was a selected for competition in the 2017 HollyShorts Screenplay Contest.

Ryan also co-wrote the upcoming Twerkfoot along with director Joe McCarthy.
